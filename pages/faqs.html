Frequently Asked Questions

Is it free?

Visual Fraction Library is completely free for classroom and personal use. If you intend to use images generated on our site to sell commercially, you will need to purchase acommercial use license starting at $10 that is good for life.

What image types are provided?

Currently our images can be downloaded as SVG's (scalable vector graphics) or PNG's (portable network graphics). They can also be copied and pasted into powerpoint, google docs, canva, etc as PNG's.

How can I help?

Reach out tovisualfractionlibrary@gmail.comif you are interested in partnering with me. I am looking for people who can help promote the site, guest blogs related to creating and using visuals to teach math, and more.

What if I notice an error or bug?

Please email me atvisualfractionlibrary@gmail.comif you find anything that might need fixing.

Can I request a new feature?

Yes, absolutely! I am happy to add new features if they fit the scope of the website and would be useful to people. Please email any feature requests tovisualfractionlibrary@gmail.com. You can also join my email list where I will send out polls for which feature I should work on next.
