Frequently Asked Questions

Is it free?

Visual Fraction Library is completely free for classroom and personal use. If you intend to use images generated on our site to sell commercially, you will need to make a one time donation to recieve a commercial use license.

What image types are provided?

Currently our images can be downloaded as SVG's (scalable vector graphics) or PNG's (portable network graphics). They can also be copied and pasted into powerpoint, google docs, canva, etc as PNG's.

How can I help?

Reach out to support@visualfractionlibrary.com if you are interested in partnering with us. We are looking for people who can help promote the site, guest blogs related to creating and using visuals to teach math, and more.

When will it be released?

We are on track to release the site around mid-september. We are in the process of conducting research and user testing to ensure our product meets all of your needs. If you would like to help shape the website or participate in user testing, fill out thissurvey.
